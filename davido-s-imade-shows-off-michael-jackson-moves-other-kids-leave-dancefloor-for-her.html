Davidos Imade Shows Off Michael Jackson Moves, Other Kids Leave Dancefloor for Her

  • Singer Davido's first child, Imade is a carefree soul, just like her father and she recently got people laughing on social media
  • The little girl was at a small party with some other kids and she took over the dancefloor with her breakdancing
  • While some people found Imade's steps hilarious and likened her to her father, other people say that she reminds them of BBNaija star, Liquorose

Looks like Davido's first child, Imade has inherited his carefree spirit especially when she is in the midst of people or just having fun.

In a video sighted on Instagram, the six-year-old was at a small party with some other kids and their parents.

Imade who was dancing with some other kids on the dancefloor suddenly decided to go Michael Jackson on them as she started breakdancing.

The other kids in the twinkle of an eye, disappeared from the dancefloor as Imade took over and her mum kept hailing her.
