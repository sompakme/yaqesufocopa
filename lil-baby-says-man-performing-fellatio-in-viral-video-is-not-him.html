Lil Baby Says Man Performing Fellatio In Viral Video Is Not Him

When you are a famous person like Lil Baby, there are going to be people who try to bring you down. Moreover, there are going to be people who may look like you or even sound like you, who will use that likeness for nefarious reasons. Well, Baby was subjected to that this weekend. If you were on Twitter last night, then you were probably aware of an NSFW video making the rounds. In the clip, a man was giving another man fellatio. Now, this typically wouldn't be anything to write home about. That was until people started claiming the man performing the act was Lil Baby.

Of course, the reactions to this possibility were far-reaching. Pretty well everyone was trying to give their take on it. From DJ Akademiks to the average fan, Lil Baby was trending on Twitter. He has been subjected to rumors about his sexuality before. Therefore, he felt like he needed to address some things before it all got too out of hand. In the tweet below, you can find a reposted Instagram story in which the artist sets the record straight.

Lil Baby Speaks

"Y'all gotta stop using my name and likeness when you get bored," Lil Baby wrote. "The extremes mfs go to for clout is sick. Ain't no mystery in my history on NO LEVEL. This my last time addressing any kind of click bait. Tf is wrong with y'all?" Needless to say, the artist was not happy with what was being showcased on social media. Overall, it is easy to see why he would feel so strongly about this. After all, it is never fun to have false rumors spreading about you.
