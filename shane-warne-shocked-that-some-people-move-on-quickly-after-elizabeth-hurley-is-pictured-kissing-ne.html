Shane Warne shocked that 'some people move on quickly' after Elizabeth Hurley is pictured kissing ne

    Celebrity fans were surprised when newly-single Elizabeth Hurley was pictured kissing a wealthy businessman-turned photographer. But seemingly, no-one was more surprised than her ex fiancé Shane Warne.The Aussie cricketer expressed shock after a friend sent him some photographs – presumably of the actress and model in a clinch with her new love interest David Yarrow. 

    Shane also joked he would join a dating app, as his friends had advised him.

    He posted on Twitter: "Was just sent some pictures, wow, some people move on quickly. Maybe now it's time to take up my single friends suggestions & join tinder!"Elizabeth was photographed sharing a late night smooth with David – a wildlife photographer who made his fortune as a hedge fund tycoon.

    The pair embraced on London's Kings Road last Thursday after the London after she attended the Tusk Charity Event at Claridges.

"FTR, I did not split up with EH because of my ex-wife, secret phone or that I didn't set a wedding date," Shane recently clarified on Twitter.
