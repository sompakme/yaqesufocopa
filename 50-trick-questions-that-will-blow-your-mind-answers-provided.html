50+ trick questions that will blow your mind (answers provided)

Trick questions are great for challenging our minds. A great set of confusing questions allows us to think harder, and at the same time, laugh at the simple answers we may fail to think about. You can incorporate such questions during a game night, date night with someone you have known for a while, or just to share a laugh with a friend.

The true beauty of tricky questions is that the answers are usually incredibly simple. A quick tip to getting the correct answers is to avoid overthinking. Do not allow your mind to magnify the challenge.
