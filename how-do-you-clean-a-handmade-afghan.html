How do you clean a handmade afghan?

Answer :

How to wash an Afghan:

Use your hands.

Select a laundry detergent. For practically all afghans, a little detergent in cold water is the most effective method of cleaning.

Wash. Make sure that you do not drag, rub, or scrub the yarn with the laundry detergent.

To finish, rinse thoroughly with cold water in the sink or tub.

Laying your crochet afghan flat on top of a towel will help to dry it.
