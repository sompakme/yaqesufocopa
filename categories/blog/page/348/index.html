How to Nail the Sleek High Ponytail Look

published on July 22, 2024
In terms of hairstyles, few are more impactful than the sleek high ponytail. Is it possible to put too much olive oil in your ear?

published on July 22, 2024
Olive oil is generally safe to use in small quantities in the ear. However, people with a ruptured eardrum should never put olive oil or other substances in the ear. Irrigating the ear with olive oil can cause side effects such as itching, outer ear infections, dizziness, and skin irritation. Can olive oil make ear wax worse? Over time, olive oil actually increased the amount of ear wax.
