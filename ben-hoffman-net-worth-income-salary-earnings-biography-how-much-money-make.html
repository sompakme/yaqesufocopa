Benjamin Isaac Hoffman (born December 1974) is a stand-up comedian and writer from the United States. He developed and presented The Ben Show, which debuted on Comedy Central on February 28, 2013, and has since gone on to win many awards. Sadly, Comedy Central decided to discontinue the programme after just one season. A former writer and reporter for the Sports Show with Norm Macdonald on Comedy Central, Hoffman has also contributed to the MTV Movie Awards and the MTV Music Awards. He has appeared on television shows such as New Girl and Drunk History, and he has created material for Current TV’s infoMania channel. Hoffman is a native of the state of Kentucky. He is the elder brother of Scissor Sisters bassist Babydaddy, who is also a member of the band (Scott Hoffman).
