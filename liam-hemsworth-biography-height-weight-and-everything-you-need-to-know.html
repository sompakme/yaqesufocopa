Liam Hemsworth Biography, Height, Weight, And Everything You Need To Know

Liam Hemsworth is an Australian actor who gained international fame for his role as Gale Hawthorne in “The Hunger Games” film series. Here’s a biography that includes information about his height, weight, and other details:

Liam Hemsworth Biography:

Liam Hemsworth was born on January 13, 1990, in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. He comes from a family of actors; his older brothers, Chris and Luke Hemsworth, are also actors. Liam began his acting career in Australian television shows before making his way to Hollywood.

He gained significant recognition for his portrayal of Gale Hawthorne, the best friend of the main character, in “The Hunger Games” film series, based on the novels by Suzanne Collins. The success of these movies propelled Liam into the spotlight and opened up opportunities for him in the entertainment industry.

Apart from “The Hunger Games,” Liam has appeared in several other films, including “The Last Song” (opposite Miley Cyrus), “Independence Day: Resurgence,” and “The Dressmaker.” He has also ventured into television, starring in the series “Westworld.”

Liam Hemsworth Height and Weight:

Liam Hemsworth stands at approximately 6 feet 3 inches (191 cm) tall. Regarding his weight, it is estimated to be around 196 pounds (89 kg). However, these measurements may vary slightly as individuals’ physical attributes can change over time.

Liam Hemsworth Personal Life:

Liam Hemsworth has had a high-profile personal life. He was in an on-again, off-again relationship with American singer and actress Miley Cyrus, whom he met while filming “The Last Song.” The couple got engaged in 2012 but eventually ended their relationship in 2013. They reconciled and married in December 2018 but announced their separation in August 2019.

Liam Hemsworth Other Interests:

Liam Hemsworth has shown an interest in various activities outside of acting. He is an avid surfer and enjoys spending time in the water. He has also been involved in philanthropic endeavors, supporting causes such as the Australian Childhood Foundation.

Liam Hemsworth continues to pursue his acting career, taking on diverse roles and showcasing his talent on the big screen.
