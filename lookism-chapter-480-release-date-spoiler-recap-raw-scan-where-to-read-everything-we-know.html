Lookism Chapter 480:- Lookism is a popular webtoon by Park Tae-joon that explores the themes of appearance, identity, and society. The story follows Daniel Park, a high school student who has a perfect body and a secret. He can switch between his original, overweight body and his handsome, fit body at will. He uses his new body to experience a different life, but he soon realizes that there are consequences and dangers to his ability.

The webtoon has been running since 2014 and has over 400 chapters. It has also been adapted into a Netflix animated series in 2021. The webtoon is updated every Sunday on WEBTOON, where you can read it for free. He had also been looking for Daniel, who had gone missing after the fight with Vasco. He finally found Daniel’s location, thanks to a tip from Zack, another friend of Daniel. He rushed to the scene, hoping to find Daniel and help him.

However, he arrived too late. He saw Daniel lying on the ground, covered in blood and bruises. He was shocked and worried, wondering what had happened to him. He tried to wake him up, but there was no response. He then noticed that Daniel’s body was changing. He saw his face and body morphing from his handsome, fit appearance to his original, overweight appearance. He realized that Daniel had a secret, and that he was not who he seemed to be. He was stunned and confused, not knowing what to make of it. The chapter ended with a cliffhanger, leaving the readers in suspense and anticipation for the next chapter.
