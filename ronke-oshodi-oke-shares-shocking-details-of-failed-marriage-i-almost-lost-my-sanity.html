Ronke Oshodi Oke Shares Shocking Details of Failed Marriage: I Almost Lost My Sanity

  • Veteran Yoruba actress Ronke Oshodi Oke has revealed her horrible experience at the hands of her ex-husband
  • The movie star revealed she almost went crazy and she was prevented from running into the street without her clothes
  • Ronke also added that she dated the said ex-husband for ten years, and the marriage did not last a year

Ronke Oshodi Oke graced colleague Biola Adebayo's Talk to B show and shared shocking details of her failed marriage.

In the short clip shared by Adebayo, the actress revealed that she dated her ex-husband for a decade, and their marriage did not last for a year.

Stating some reasons for the unfortunate end of her union, Ronke noted that there was a difference between a man who did not have money and one who just did not care.
