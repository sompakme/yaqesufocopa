The Apothecary Diaries, known as Kusuriya no Hitorigoto, tells a fascinating story through beautiful visuals and an engaging plot. Created by Natsu Hyūga and illustrated by Touko Shino, this series has entranced readers since 2011 on the Shōsetsuka ni Narō website.

Maomao’s Journey Unveiled

Set in the bustling city of Edo, the tale orbits around Maomao, a young woman with a mysterious past and remarkable abilities. Originally aiming for a medical career, her life takes a twist when she’s forced to serve in the Imperial Harem after being kidnapped. To blend in, she tries to stay unnoticed among other servants.

A Curious Turn of Events

However, tranquility fades as rumors spread about a curse plaguing the Emperor’s heirs, leading to the death of each newborn. Only a 3-month-old Prince and a 6-month-old Princess survive. Identifying the curse’s origin, Maomao takes it upon herself to find a cure, catching the attention of high-ranking officials and adding complexity to her life.

Chapter 66 Release Date

For avid fans eagerly awaiting Chapter 66 of Kusuriya no Hitorigoto, the wait is nearly over. Set to release in the Young Ace magazine on December 4, 2023, the chapter will go live online at midnight in Japan Standard Time (GMT +9). To grab a copy, interested readers can purchase the magazine through its official website. The release date for the January 2024 issue will follow the availability of the December edition.

Note on Upcoming Chapters

There won’t be new chapters in both the November and December issues of Young Ace, as per the author’s announcement. This notice pertains exclusively to the original Japanese chapters and won’t affect the translated versions.
