Celebrity stylist Swanky Jerry causes buzz as he rocks interesting outfit

- Swanky Jerry recently left social media buzzing with reactions following his recent photo

- The celebrity stylist recently rocked a black flowing unique outfit

- Many people including celebrities have reacted to the look

Jeremiah Ogbodo, professionally known as Swanky Jerry, sure knows how to get Nigerians talking. The stylist who is known for his eccentric fashion sense never misses an opportunity to leave mouths hanging.

In a recent post to his Instagram page, the 28-year-old posed in a black outfit. While many people believe jumpsuits that aren't work-related, are mostly for women, Swanky Jerry seems to think otherwise.

In the photo, the stylist who was in Rosebank, Guateng, struck a pose in a stylish outfit that looks like a jumpsuit, and it didn't take long before his comment section was flooded with many people including celebrities.
