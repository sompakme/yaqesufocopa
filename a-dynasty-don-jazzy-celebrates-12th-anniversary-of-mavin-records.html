&quot;A Dynasty&quot;: Don Jazzy Celebrates 12th Anniversary of Mavin Records

  • Top record producer, Michael Collins Ajereh, popularly known as Don Jazzy is celebrating his record label at 12
  • The multi-talent founded his label, Mavin Records, in 2012 after parting ways with his jointly run label, Mo'hits
  • Jazzy shared a music video of Mavin all-stars, spanning from the time of Tiwa Savage to Di'ja, Reekado Banks, and others
  • In a chat with Legit.ng, Content strategist, Tunmise recounts his experience working with the Mavin boss so far and expressed appreciation

Influential Nigerian music producer and label boss Michael Collins Ajereh, professionally known as Don Jazzy, is celebrating his label's 12th anniversary.

The recording company was founded in 2012, with founding members being ace music stars like Tiwa Savage, Korede Bello, Reekado Banks, and Dr Sid, to mention a few.

Don Jazzy, who recently linked up with his friend and brother, D'banj, is in a celebratory mood and has shared nostalgic music videos released by old and new-school Mavin Record signees.

It will be recalled that a former Mavin record signee, Reekado Banks, disclosed what transpired between him and his former boss, Don Jazzy, after he left the label.

Speaking in a podcast with media personality Ifedayo Olarinde, aka Daddy Freeze, Reekado said:

"That man took me from absolutely nothing. I wonder why people would think that there was a problem. There never was. Everything was amicable. The end of the contract came, I wanted to leave and he gave me his blessings. I feel it is a tradition in the industry for artists to break out angrily from a record label, and I feel that is being imposed on our relationship."

Tunmise applauds Don Jazzy, details working with him

Talented content writer and strategist Tunmise Olasokan fondly called the black bad guy in a chat with Eniola of Legit.ng shouted out to Don Jazzy for holding down the fort for so long.

According to him, no one has ever done it.

"Maintaining a record label at the highest level for 12 years is such an incredible feat. Especially because they’ve produced top artistes every single year, even up until now.
