What exactly does an echocardiography detect?

Echocardiography (echo) is a procedure that uses sound waves to see the size, shape, and movement of different sections of your heart. The heart valves, the septum (the wall dividing the right and left heart chambers), and the walls of the heart chambers are among the components of the heart.
