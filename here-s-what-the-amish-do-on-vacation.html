Here's What The Amish Do On Vacation

Since they don't often have a lot of contact with people outside their communities, there's a lot of misconceptions about the Amish. Media representations of them are rare and the few portrayals of them, such as in the '80s movie "Witness," or even reality TV shows like "Breaking Amish," haven't helped to educate more. There's a lot that people still don't know when it comes to the Amish, but truth be told, they live life like everyone else â€” just under a different structure. That includes normal things like vacationing. So what does a vacation look like for the Amish?

As mentioned earlier, the Amish do not travel by driving motorized vehicle themselves. However, they do use buses, trains, and will accept a ride in a car (taxi), so long as they are the passenger, per Amish America.
