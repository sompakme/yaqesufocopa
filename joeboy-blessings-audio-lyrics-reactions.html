Joeboy - Blessings: audio, lyrics, reactions

The song by Joeboy - Blessings was released on 7th November 2019, and the song is the fifth in his latest EP christened Love & Light. Well, if you thought that Joeboy is all about love songs, then this tune comes as a surprise because he shoves love aside for a moment and requests for favour from God.

If you love Afrobeats music, waste no more time and get the details about this new track. This is one of the many songs that display the prowess of the talented musician and make him a leading artist in the industry. Create time and listen to this nice piece.

Joeboy - Blessings description

  • Release date: 7th November 2019
  • Format: Audio
  • Genre: Afropop
  • Length: 2.30 minutes
  • Label: Banku Music

Even though Joeboy is known for releasing tracks whose central theme is love, this time, he comes up with something quite peculiar. This song inspires hope and the artist heaps a lot of praise on God for lots of good things in his lifetime. He particularly asks for God’s protection and guidance in his endeavors. It is truly an inspirational song with powerful lyrics that give hope to many people.
