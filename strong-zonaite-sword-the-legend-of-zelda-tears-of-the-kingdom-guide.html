Strong Zonaite Sword - The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom Guide

The Strong Zonaite Sword is a single-handed weapon in The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom. This weapon is just a normal sword made out of zonaite. You should attach other zonaite devices to it to increase its attack power.

Strong Zonaite Sword Stats

A strong Zonai sword made of zonaite. It resonates with attached Zonai devices to increase some of its attack power.
