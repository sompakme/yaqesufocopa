&quot;Wizkid Go Still Sell Out&quot;: Paulo Advises Afrobeats Artists, Urges Them to Reduce Ticket Prices

  • A Nigerian show promoter and music executive, Paul Okoye, also known as Oga Paulo, has dished out a word of advice to Afrobeats artists
  • The father-of-two shared his thoughts about the happenings in the industry via his Instagram story
  • As stated by him, "2024 is going to be a tough year" for artists beyond the C class as the global economy is not favourable

Nigerian music executive, Paul Okoye, also known as Oga Paulo, has tendered heartfelt advice to Nigerian artists of the Afrobeats genre.

There have been numerous complaints from fans about the hike in concert ticket prices. This has made it difficult for shows to sell out, as music lovers cannot afford to watch their favourites perform at the expense of their finances.

Paulo admonished Nigerian artists to stop over-pricing themselves so that they could have successful and sold-out shows.
