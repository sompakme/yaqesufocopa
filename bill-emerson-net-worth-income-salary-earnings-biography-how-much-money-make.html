Norvell William “Bill” Emerson (January 1, 1938 – June 22, 1996) was a Missouri politician who served in the United States Senate. From 1981 until his death in 1996, he was a member of the House of Representatives of the United States. Jo Ann Emerson, his widow, succeeded him as a member of the House of Representatives. Emerson was a member of the Republican Party.
