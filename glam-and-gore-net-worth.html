About

Also known as Mykie, she is a professional beauty and special effects makeup Artist who showcases her craft on YouTube and Instagram. She features foundational teaching, product reviews, industry advice and a mix of special effects and avant-garde makeup tutorials on her channel.

Before Fame

She is a self-taught makeup guru. She launched her YouTube channel on March 16, 2014 and posted a "Mykie welcomes you to Glam&Gore!" video on April 15, 2014.

Trivia

She reached 1 million subscribers on YouTube in October 2015. Her "How to do the Kylie Jenner challenge SAFELY" video garnered more than 800,000 videos in just one month.

Family Life

Her real name is Lauren Mychal; but she goes by Mykie. She was born and raised in Philadelphia before moving to Los Angeles for her career.
