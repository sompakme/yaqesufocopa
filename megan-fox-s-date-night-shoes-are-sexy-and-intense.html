Megan Fox's Date Night Shoes Are Sexy and Intense

Right now, some of us may shudder at the idea of slipping into platform heels or extra-high stilettos, but not Megan Fox. The actress and lover of monochromatic looks seems to have an extensive collection of statement-making shoes, and she has no problem breaking them out for date night with her boyfriend, Machine Gun Kelly.

For over the year, the two have treated us to enviable couple outfits, whether they're layering with trench coats or adding splashes of pink. However, once we're done marveling at how in sync these two are while heading to dinner, we tend to focus on Fox's footwear next. Her heels don't just add height and a fancy twist, they also increase the sexiness and general sense of intensity of her outfits by about tenfold. If you love a classic, understated pump, you're going to want to move along — the shoe inspo here is bolder, brighter, and sure maybe intimidating-er than all of your everyday go-tos.

Ahead, we've rounded up Fox's best date night shoes, along with similar options for you to shop.

A Pop of Pink

We enjoyed seeing this couple coordinate their trench coats, but what made Fox's light pink version even better was that she stuck with a theme, choosing to style it with bright pink pumps.

Shop now: Balenciaga Square Knife Pointed Toe Pump; $850

Studded and Not-So-Simple

If Fox has a go-to pair of heels, these illusion naked sandals are it. They're kind of the best of both worlds, since they include sparkly stud details but are partially clear, ensuring they're versatile while still packing a punch.

Shop now: Alexander Wang Nova Studded PCV Slingback Sandals; $595

Here's the same pair working overtime to elevate more casual items, such as flared black pants and a denim jacket.

Cool Cake Stands

No one nails the cake stand heel trend better than Amina Muaddi — the flared bottom design is the brand's signature. While out with Machine Gun Kelly, Fox chose to amp up a little black dress with a yellow pair, instantly transforming a simple combination into something special.

Shop similar: Amina Muaddi Naima Strappy Sandal; $985

Big and Chunky

An easy way to make jeans feel exciting again? Over-the-top footwear. While some might worry about balancing in such a high, intense pair of platforms (hi, it's us), Fox got the assist from MGK, who helped her over a curb.

Shop similar: Tom Ford Velvet and Zebra Effect Crystal Stones Sisco Platform Sandal; $3,950

Something Wild

With life slowly returning to normal around the United States, we've been embracing the chaotic bottom trend — and in some cases, that means wearing prints from hip to heel. Fox reminded us that you can't go wrong with piling on the cheetah, and dared us to mix more classic options with colorful ones, giving things an unexpected twist.

Shop Similar: Stuart Weitzman Ellsie 100 Cheetah-Print Strappy Stiletto Sandals; $450
