Paul Wesley and Torrey DeVitto Announce Divorce

Another Vampire Diaries romance has been drained of its life blood.

A few months after Ian Somerhalder and Nina Dobrev shocked fans by calling it quits, a rep for Paul Wesley confirms to People that this actor and Torrey DeVitto are filing for divorce.

Paul, 31, and Torrey, 29, “have decided to amicably split. They will continue to remain good friends,” says the Wesley insider.

No further details were provided on the couple’s breakup.

 

TVD star Wesley and DeVitto were married in a private New York City ceremony in April 2011, four years after meeting on the set of Killer Movie.

The actress recurred on Season 3 of The Vampire Diaries and is also known for roles on Pretty Little Liars and Army Wives.

We wish them the best in what is sure to be a difficult time.

Both are very good looking. They should rebound quickly.
