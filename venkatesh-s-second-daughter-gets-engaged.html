Venkatesh's second daughter gets engaged!

Wedding bells are on the horizon for the Daggubati family, as Venkatesh’s second daughter, Hayavahini, is gearing up to exchange vows. The media is buzzing with anticipation over the impending marriage as she prepares to unite with the family of a doctor hailing from Vijayawada.

Reports suggest that the engagement ceremony is slated for today in Vijayawada. Sources indicate that the Daggubati family has already embarked on their journey to partake in this joyous celebration. The event is expected to be an intimate gathering, attended by close relatives from both sides.

Venkatesh, known for his minimal presence on social media, has consistently upheld a philosophy that allows his children to make independent choices, including their career paths.

This year, he made his debut in the world of streaming platforms with the web series “Rana Naidu.”

Presently, Venkatesh is engrossed in his work on the film “Saindhav,” where he assumes the role of the protagonist. Sailesh Kolanu is the film’s director.
