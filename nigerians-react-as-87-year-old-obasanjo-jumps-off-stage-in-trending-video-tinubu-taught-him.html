Nigerians React As 87-Year-Old Obasanjo Jumps off Stage in Trending Video: Tinubu Taught Him

  • Former President Olusegun Obasanjo does not really look and act like his age mates, and he recently convinced many that he was still active
  • The ex-Nigerian leader got many talking on social media following his action at an event in Lagos state
  • In reaction, some Nigerians noted that Tinubu could not do the same while others said Obasanjo no doubt was fit even at 87

Legit.ng journalist Esther Odili has over two years of experience covering political parties and movements.

Lagos state - Former President Olusegun Obasanjo has got Nigerians talking on social media, especially X.

In a video that has gone viral, the crowd went wild and was excited as Obasanjo jumped off a stage.

The former president had given a speech at the 9th International Trade Exhibition and Conference on Agrofood, Plastics, Printing, and Packaging in Lagos state on Tuesday, March 26, when he surprised many with his move, showing that he was still very much active.

Obasanjo's advisory to Tinubu's govt

Meanwhile, ex-president Obasanjo revealed what President Bola Tinubu's administration needs to alleviate the ongoing economic hardship.

Obasanjo said the economic hardship is caused by the mismanagement of resources and lack of productivity among Nigerians.

He said Tinubu's administration should devolve power and resources from the federal government to state and local governments.
