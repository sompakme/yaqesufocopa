Northwest Arkansas Mall Fayetteville: Everything You Need to Know About Store Hours

Are you planning to visit the Northwest Arkansas Mall in Fayetteville but not sure about the store’s opening and closing hours? With countless shops, eateries, and entertainment options, the mall is the perfect spot to indulge in shopping, dining, and having fun. Read on to learn more about the shopping hours, holiday hours, and some helpful tips to make the most of your visit to Northwest Arkansas Mall.

What Time Does the Northwest Arkansas Mall Open and Close?

The mall opens from Monday to Saturday at 10:00 am and closes at 9:00 pm. On Sundays, the mall opens at 12:00 pm and closes at 6:00 pm. However, these hours may vary for different stores and restaurants within the mall. It’s always wise to double-check the timings of the stores you plan to visit.

Is Northwest Arkansas Mall Open on Weekends?

Yes, the Northwest Arkansas Mall is open on weekends. On Saturdays, the mall opens its doors at 10:00 am and closes at 9:00 pm, while on Sundays, the mall opens an hour late at 12:00 pm and closes at 6:00 pm.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to make the most of your visit to the Northwest Arkansas Mall:

– Check the mall’s website or social media channels for the latest holiday hours, deals, and promotions before you visit.
– Arrive early in the day to avoid long lines and crowds.
– Wear comfortable shoes as you will be doing a lot of walking.
– If you plan to shop at specific stores, check their timings beforehand to avoid disappointment.
– Take advantage of the mall’s free Wi-Fi to stay connected and get updates on sales and promotions.
– Make use of the mall’s phone charging stations and ATMs for your convenience.
– Park near the entrance closest to the store you plan to visit to save time and effort.
