You Be Killin Em

Give You Be Killin Em (Prod By Ryan Leslie) a listen - it's a new offering from Fabolous. It was dropped on Sunday, August 29th, 2010. Fabolous's musical situation is improving with every new release, and You Be Killin Em (Prod By Ryan Leslie) will most definitely help the cause. It's a fine addition to the growing body of work Fabolous has been developing since stepping foot in the game. We're looking forward to what Fabolous has in store.

Hear more of Fabolous on Funkmaster Flex Freestyle. Check out Fabolous's profile page for the most recent info, news, songs, and mixtapes.
