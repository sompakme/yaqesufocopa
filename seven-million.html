Seven Million

On his 22nd birthday, Lil Uzi Vert surprised fans with the release of The Perfect Luv Tape, the follow-up to April's highly acclaimed Lil Uzi Vert vs. the World. It had been a while since a mixtape of this caliber had impacted the summer. In fact, the last tape with this much hype was probably DJ Esco's Project E.T. Just like that one, The Perfect Luv Tape includes a collaboration between Uzi and Future. "Seven Million" is the tape's final track. It's a special one. 

Like "Too Much Sauce," "Seven Million" is a particularly placid sound for both collaborators. They're still flexing hard, though there's something sentimental about the manner in which they do so. The drums are electric, but the soundscape is given a soothing quality thanks to the atmospheric synths. The production is a collaboration between Nard & B, XL, and Don Cannon.
