Kim Kardashian receives matching chains for Kanye West and unborn son

    Like father, like son.... Kim Kardashian and Kanye West's son will inherit his dad's unique style judging by the gifts he has already received. The expectant mum posted a picture of a gift she had received for her unborn son on Thursday – an identical diamond-encrusted gold chain to match his dad's.

    "#LikeFatherLikeSon Thank you so much @lorenridinger @jrridinger @amberridinger @duanemclaughlin for the chain to match daddy's!" Kim had written.

    View post on Instagram 

    Kim Kardashian received an identical chain to Kanye West's for their unborn son

    The chain was a gift from the Ridinger family, who are close friends of Kim and Kanye and celebrated Fourth of July with the couple earlier this year.

    Kim is expected to give birth in December, with sources previously telling that the reality star is due on Christmas Day. The 35-year-old is clearly excited about having a baby brother for her daughter North, and recently celebrated with a baby shower hosted by her close family and friends.

    The celebrations were originally cancelled after Khloé Kardashian's husband Lamar Odom was admitted to hospital in a critical condition, however Kim went ahead with the bash after he began showing signs of recovery. Hosted at the home of family friend Irving Azoff, the themed celebrations involved Kim gathering with friends and family including sisters Kourtney, Khloé, Kendall and Kylie in matching striped pyjamas for a fun and relaxing "Sister Sunday".

    View post on Instagram 

    Kim and Kanye's baby boy is due in December

    It has been a busy couple of weeks for the star, who also celebrated her 35th birthday in October. After husband Kanye hosted a quirky themed party where guests all wore fake baby bumps to look like her, Kim invited a selection of her biggest fans to join her for a special brunch in Los Angeles a few days later.

    One fan, Charlotte Heyes from Manchester, was flown over to the US for the meeting which she told HELLO! Online was "simply amazing".

    "Kim stayed with us a lot longer than I expected and the experience was something I'll never forget," said Charlotte. "It was simply amazing!"
