Early Life and Career

The Powerlist is an annual ranking that honors the accomplishments and influence of individuals of African or African Caribbean descent in the UK.

Edward Enninful, a British editor and stylist of Ghanaian origin, was born on February 22, 1972, in Ghana. His journey in the fashion industry began at the tender age of 16 when he embarked on a modeling career with i-D magazine. Rapidly, his exceptional talent and unwavering passion propelled him into the role of fashion director at the same publication.
