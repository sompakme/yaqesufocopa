Can I put a sealer over latex paint?

Answer

The use of latex paint normally does not need the use of additional sealers. Applying a clear topcoat over a latex-painted surface, on the other hand, aids in sealing and protecting the surface area. Clear sealants may also be used to offer an extra layer of protection to faux-painted walls.

Is it possible to apply a clear coat over latex paint in the same way?

Polyurethane comes in two forms: oil-based and water-based. You may apply either type over latex paint; however, oil-based polyurethane tends to yellow with time, whilst water-based variants retain their transparency over time.

Is it possible to apply wax over latex paint in the same way?

When using Latex paint, wax as a top coat is not recommended since, as you may be aware, Latex has a rubbery surface that cannot be penetrated by wax. Latex paint simply floats on the surface, which is why it is always necessary to use a primer before applying Latex paint to ensure that the paint has something to “bite” into.
