7 things you DIDNT KNOW about Love Islands Sam Bird Thu 21 June 2018 13:00 Updated Fri

Another Bombshell has raced into Majorca with Sam Bird joining the Love Island 2018 cast.

The fitness buff is set to raise the temperature around the villa with two singles eager to catch his eye.

Bird is officially the word for Love Island’s Rosie Williams and Samira Mighty.

https://twitter.com/caitlynvanbeckx/status/1009541923739262976

Screen Shot: Love Island’s Sam Bird – ITV2, Series 4, episode 15

1. Sam Is Not A Professional Racecar Driver

Google Sam Bird and you’re likely to stumble across a Formula E and Ferrari GTE driver called Sam Bird.

This is not the geezer on Love Island.

Rumours snowballed across Twitter as the type of people who view Lad Bible as a reliable source of news started to believe that racecar driver Sam Bird was now in the villa.

Not quite…

https://twitter.com/sambirdracing/status/1009545330503966721

2. He Was Georgia Toffolo’s Personal Trainer

As spotted on Instagram, Sam was the man to whip Made in Chelsea star Georgia Toffolo into shape before her 2017 victory in I’m a Celebrity… Get Me Out of Here.

The pair budded up at Georgia’s London apartment, although we can’t imagine they train together now considering Sam lives in Norwich.

https://www.instagram.com/p/BYocSGnhJ1J/?hl=en&taken-by=samrobertbird

3. Sam Is Half-decent at Football

We say half-decent as this the fairest analysis of Sam’s exploits on the pitch.

Sam plays more on the Sunday league scale although his team, Dussindale Rovers, recently won the Norfolk Junior Cup, held at Norwich City’s Carrow Road.

https://www.instagram.com/p/BifeF-rhmdx/?hl=en&taken-by=samrobertbird

4. The Public Do Not Like His Eyebrows

Although Sam featured in Love Island episode 15 for all of 15 seconds, that didn’t stop fans from instantly picking up a pet hate.

Twitter went into meltdown over Sam’s ‘non-symmetrical’ eyebrows.

At least Hayley Hughes isn’t around to spend a solid hour chatting about eyebrow colour, volume, density and shape.

5. Sam Is A Personal Trainer

The second personal trainer to join the ranks, alongside Adam Collard, we can’t wait to hear the pair bro-down over the optimum time to digest protein.

Sam works as a head trainer and class specialist for boutique ‘disco-gym’ Train With Core, located in Norwich.

It looks like a right hoot…

https://www.instagram.com/p/Bj7LMySAXYq/?taken-by=coregymnorwich

6. His Puns Are Totally Cringe

Sam is the first Love Island series four contestants to storm into the villa boasting his own nickname.

Apparently, Sam is known as ‘The Bird’.

Fully embracing his pun-able surname, Sam said: When The Bird’s got his eagle eye out, no-one is safe.”

View Tweet

7. Sam Is Well Travelled

Perhaps a bit of a party boy, Sam’s Instagram is full of him enjoying shots in Ibiza.

It’s not just the White Isle where Sam has been living it up, however, also spotted dotting across the U.S.A and enjoying the high life in Dubai.
