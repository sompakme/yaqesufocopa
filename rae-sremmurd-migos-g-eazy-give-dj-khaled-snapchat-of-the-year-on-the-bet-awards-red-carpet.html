Rae Sremmurd, Migos &amp; G-Eazy Give DJ Khaled &quot;Snapchat Of The Year&quot; On The BET Awards Red Carpet

This year's BET went down on Sunday, and the biggest stars in rap and R&B made it out for the occasion. HotNewHipHop was on the red carpet, where we caught up with a few artists who shared their opinions on a few award categories that weren't a part of the show, but we felt needed to be recognized. Migos, Rae Sremmurd, Yo Gotti, G-Eazy, and BJ The Chicago Kid weighed in on Best Snapchat (Khaled took it unanimously), Biggest Come-Up Of The Year, Best Bars, and Best Dressed. Many of the artists couldn't resist giving themselves some of the awards, and naturally, there are some hilarious moments, including the Sremm Bros' recollection of Khaled's infamous "lost at sea" Snapchat story.
