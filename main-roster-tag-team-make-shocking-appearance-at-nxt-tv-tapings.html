Main Roster Tag Team Make Shocking Appearance at NXT TV Tapings

The world of professional wrestling is unpredictable as you never know who could appear on any given show in WWE. That is exactly what happened during the NXT TV tapings this week, as a main roster tag team made their presence felt.

NXT recorded the next episode of the show on February 20, 2024, following its recent broadcast. The episode featured significant matches and segments. This is also where Shotzi suffered an unfortunate injury.

A fan took to Twitter and uploaded photos from the tapings for the next episode, revealing the return of two-time RAW Tag Team Champions Luke Gallows and Karl Anderson to the NXT brand after 490 days. They made their comeback during a segment, laying out Chase U.

The images depict Gallows and Anderson standing tall, striking their signature pose, while members of Chase U are sprawled out on the mat. Nonetheless, this definitely made fans wonder what the OC will even do in NXT.

We will have to wait and see whether Luke Gallows and Karl Anderson will end up having an extended run with Chase U from next week, as the duo have been largely directionless after AJ Styles broke away from The OC.
