According to the World Bank, the aluminum price will increase to $2,000 per metric ton (t) in 2021, a 17% rise from an average of $1,703/t in 2020, and experience moderate growth to $2,050/t in 2022. By 2035, the World Bank predicts aluminum prices will reach $2,400/t. Will aluminium prices go up? Aluminium Prices Are Likely to Rise and Then Fall throughout 2022. Why are aluminum prices increasing? The main reasons for the hike in aluminum are substantial increase in energy and raw material costs, growing demand, decline in China's production capacity, reduction in global inventories and the impact of COVID-19.
