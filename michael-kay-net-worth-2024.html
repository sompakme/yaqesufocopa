Michael Kay was born and raised in the Bronx, New York. He developed a passion for sports at a young age, which led him to pursue a career in sports journalism. He graduated from Fordham University with a degree in communications.

Michael Kay’s career began as a writer for the New York Post. He later moved to the New York Daily News, where he covered the New York Yankees. His insightful reporting and unique style quickly caught the attention of the sports broadcasting industry.
