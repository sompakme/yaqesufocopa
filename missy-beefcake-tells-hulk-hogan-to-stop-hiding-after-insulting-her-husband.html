Missy Beefcake Tells Hulk Hogan To Stop Hiding After Insulting Her Husband

WWE Hall of Famer Hulk Hogan is one of the biggest and most influential names in the pro wrestling business and has accomplished a lot in the industry. However, Hogan has also been known for his eccentric remarks throughout his career. Now it appears Brutus Beefcake’s wife went after Hulk Hogan yet again.

Hulk Hogan might still have some beef with Brutus “The Barber” Beefcake and it has gotten worse after Brutus Beefcake’s wife Missy Beefcake involved herself in all the drama.

Missy Beefcake even challenged Hulk Hogan last month in bizarre video. Missy Beefcake then claimed that she had been silenced for too long. She took to TikTok yet again and sang her own version of Hulk Hogan’s “Real American” theme, where she told Hogan to stop hiding after he insulted her husband.

“Well the s**** coming down it’s gonna hurt inside, six foot two hair extensions are tight. Why’d you hurt my man, ’cause I hurt your pride? You broke bro-code, so don’t try and hide. You are a real s***** friend, and it’s not right…”

We will have to see what happens next in this situation, but it seems that Missy Beefcake is clearly not done going after Hulk Hogan.
