Akira Tozawa Says He Was Cute While Flaunting Mind-Blowing Physical Transformation

WWE Superstars put a lot of work into their appearance. Sometimes, that means someone changes dramatically over their life. That is certainly the case for Akira Tozawa.

Tozawa logged onto X, and he let everyone know how far he’s come. The Alpha Academy member posted a photo of himself from back in the day, along with one of him now. He also posted his thoughts about his previous look.
