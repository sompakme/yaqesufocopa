How can I invest in aluminium?

There are no direct ways to invest in aluminum, but you can invest indirectly through futures contracts, ETFs that invest in futures contracts, and the mines themselves. The latter two choices are available through the stock market.

Does aluminum trade on the stock market?

Listed under the symbol AL, aluminum is a physical commodity traded on the New York Mercantile Exchange. One futures contract controls 44,000 pounds of aluminum and one price tick up or down is worth $22.00.

Will scrap prices go up in 2021?
