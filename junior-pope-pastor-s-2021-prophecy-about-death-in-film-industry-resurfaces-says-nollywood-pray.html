Junior Pope: Pastor's 2021 Prophecy About Death in Film Industry Resurfaces, Says &quot;Nollywood Pray&quot;

  • A prophecy issued by a man of God is now trending on social media after the death of Nollywood actor Junior Pope
  • The two sets of prophecies concern actors and actresses in the Nollywood industry, as the prophet said he saw deaths lurking around
  • The prophecies were issued in 2021 and 2024, and the prophet's church is saying they were being fulfilled following Junior Pope's death

The death of Nollywood actor John Paul Odonwodo, popularly known as Junior Pope, threw Nigerians into mourning, but some people are saying the sad event has been foretold.

The Grace Confirmation Church Worldwide, a Christian organisation, insists that its prophet spoke about the death in Nollywood in 2021.

In a new Facebook post shared shortly after the sad passage of Junior Pope, the church said their prophet, General Robert, had said there would be deaths in Nollywood.

"I see death coming to cover the Nollywood industry. Nollywood, pray."

In another set of prophecies issued in 2024, the prophet restated that he was still seeing death lurking around Nollywood. This time, he was specific, mentioning names such as Mr Ibu and Amaechi Muonagor.

His words:

"I see Nollywood legends are gone. Zack Orji gone, Amaechi Munonagor gone, Mr Ibu gone."

The church insisted that the Nollywood industry should start listening to the prophet's words.

It wrote on Facebook:

"This is a prophecy of since 2021 still playing up on till date (2024). Won't the Nollywood industry pray? Won't they come seek the face of God through the prophet? Why feel too big when all lives in the industry are in danger."
