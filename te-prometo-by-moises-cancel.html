Te Prometo by Moises Cancel

MultiTracks are all of the individual parts or "stems" that make up a song. Master stems are made from the original master recording and are available in 12 keys, engineered for live performance.

MultiTracks are available for download in WAV or M4A format to use in any DAW. You can rent MultiTracks in Playback with a Playback Rentals Subscription. Each MultiTrack includes a click and guide track and you can adjust levels and mute and un-mute any track to enhance the sound of your team.

  • 12 Keys
  • Ableton Live file
  • Click & Guide
