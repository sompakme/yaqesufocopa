Na Laugh Cause This Problem? Phyna Reacts As Netizens Drag Her Over Comment About Lady Killed

  • Big Brother Naija 2022 winner Phyna has been dragged over her insensitive posts about a lady who her boyfriend allegedly killed

Not one to cower in the face of dragging, Phyna made a video and several posts defending herself and her actions.
