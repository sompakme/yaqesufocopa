Caitlyn Fitzpatrick - InStyle

Education: Monmouth University

Location: New York, New York

Expertise: Haircare

  • Caitlyn is senior shopping editor at TMB (Trusted Media Brands).
  • Previously, she was senior editor at Hearst Magazines.
  • Her writing has appeared in POPSUGAR, InStyle, and Stylecaster.

Experience

Caitlyn has worked in media for the past eight years. She has held editorial positions at Hearst, Popsugar, and Seventeen. She is currently on the shopping team at TMB where she works on Taste of Home, Reader’s Digest, and The Family Handyman.

Education

Caitlyn attended Monmouth University where she studied Communication, Journalism, Public Relations, and Interactive Media as an undergrad.
