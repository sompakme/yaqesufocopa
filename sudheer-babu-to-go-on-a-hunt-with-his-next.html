Sudheer Babu To Go On A Hunt With His Next

Article by Nanda Gopal Published on: 2:55 pm, 28 August 2022

Talented actor Sudheer Babu picks peculiar and diverse scripts every now and again. He is now turning a powerful cop for his next film which is being produced by Bhavya Creations.

The makers of this cop thriller have announced that the film has has been titled ‘Hunt’. A striking new poster was unveiled to confirm the same.

The title ‘Hunt’ could be an implication of the action-oriented theme of the film and it implies that the film is a whodunnit mystery with a police back drop.

Sudheer Babu plays a powerful cop in the film and the action sequences will be sleek and stylish. He will share the screen with Srikanth and ‘Premisthe’ fame Bharath. The makers say Sudheer’s character in the film will be unlike anything he had done till now. Hunt is directed by Mahesh and it is currently in the post production phase.

The makers will be announcing the theatrical release date soon. The film is produced by Bhavya Anand Prasad under Bhavya Creations banner.
