Seth Rollins New Look Revealed After Recent Tease

Seth Rollins has accomplished a lot during his time in WWE, and there is truly no end to what he can do in the company even now. No matter what, Rollins manages to stay relevant throughout it all.

The current United States Champion has competed against the best stars in ROH as well as WWE, honing his skills in other companies. Rollins also recently hinted at reverting back to his old look.

Wrestle Ops took to Twitter and uploaded a photo of Seth Rollins out in public, where he was seemingly sporting a different look.

Seemingly a pic of Seth Rollins’ new look…

We will have to wait and see whether Rollins will end up changing his character to go along with his new look or not. Fans are certainly excited to see what he will end up doing next.
